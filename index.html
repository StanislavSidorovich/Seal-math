Arctic Math Adventure · v1.0.0

An educational math game for children aged 6–11. Help Sausage the Seal rescue friends across the Arctic Ocean by solving math problems.

🐟 Free to play. No ads. No in-app purchases. All data stays on your device.

For feedback: sausagetheseal@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game progress is saved locally on your device only.

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

This app is designed for children. No account registration is required. No data leaves your device.
